Lumen is a fast, beautiful player for the IPTV service you already pay for. Sign in with your Xtream / X3U codes or paste an M3U playlist URL, and enjoy Live TV, Movies and Series with a clean, modern interface.

Lumen is a PLAYER only. It comes with no channels, movies or other content — you bring your own subscription or playlist.

LIVE TV
• Full TV-guide grid (channels × time) with now/next and a "jump to now" line
• Catch-up / archive playback where your provider supports it
• Optional XMLTV EPG when you use a plain M3U playlist

MOVIES & SERIES
• Rich artwork, ratings, cast and trailers (TMDB)
• Continue watching and Recently watched
• Next-episode autoplay with a countdown, plus Skip-intro

OFFLINE
• Download movies and episodes for offline viewing
• Pause / resume downloads; files saved to a real folder

A PLAYER THAT FEELS PREMIUM
• Polished video player: audio & subtitle tracks, subtitle styling, playback speed, sleep timer
• Picture-in-Picture — keep watching while you use other apps
• Floating mini-player while you browse

MAKE IT YOURS
• Light / Dark / System themes
• Choose your own accent colour — the whole UI recolours to match
• Multiple profiles, customisable Home, search with sort & filter

BUILT FOR EVERY SCREEN
• Phones and tablets
• Android TV / Google TV with full remote (D-pad) navigation
• Also available for macOS, Windows and Linux

Lumen does not host, provide or stream any content. It is a third-party client for your own legally obtained IPTV subscription or playlist. You are responsible for the sources you add.
